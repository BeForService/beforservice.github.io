📖 How to Play

The SCOR-DS Framework
SCOR-DS defines supply chain performance across 5 attributes: Reliability (RL), Responsiveness (RS), Agility (AG), Cost (CO), Asset Management (AM). It organizes activity into 7 macro-processes: Orchestrate → Source → Transform → Order → Fulfill → Return → Enable. The 4P Framework (Performance, Process, Practice, People) is the lens for analysis.
① Setup — Choose a Focus KPI (double points). Set teams, rounds, and difficulty. Difficulty scales KPI impact magnitudes at scoring time: Easy ×0.8, Medium ×1.0, Hard ×1.2.
② Each Round — A scenario tagged to its SCOR-DS process domain is presented. Teams choose a decision simultaneously (blind). Then all decisions are revealed.
③ Reveal & Debrief — Each decision reveals a SCOR-DS Insight. A Systemic Insight panel connects the scenario to the NexaFlow-type paradox. This is the core learning moment.
④ The Core Paradox — NexaFlow scenarios show that local KPI optimization destroys global performance. Watch for decisions that score green locally but red at the system level.
